Murder Mystery Minecraft World

Have you ever played a Minecraft Murder Mystery World?
We offer a free-to-play world for Java Edition. Provided as a .zip file for full transparency and security.

Step Instructions
Step 1 Un-zip the Minecraft Folder that you downloaded from us.
Step 2 Copy the un-zipped folder.
Step 3 Locate your .minecraft folder (C:/users/yourusername/AppData/.minecraft). Note: "Show Hidden Files" must be enabled.
Step 4 Go into your saves folder.
Step 5 Paste the folder there. Launch Minecraft: Java Edition and enjoy!
